The "Spoliarium"

The "Spoliarium" is a renowned painting created by Filipino artist Juan Luna in 1884. The huge piece, a masterpiece that depicts the effects of gladiator fighting in classic Rome, is in for the harsh reality of social injustice and colonial oppression that prevailed in the Philippines at the time.

The Artist

Juan Luna was a late 19th-century Filipino painter, sculptor, and political activist who participated in the Philippine Revolution. He increased to popularity as one of the first known Filipino artists.
