Rizal Monument

The Rizal Monument in Manila's Rizal Park is a symbolic tribute to Dr. Jose Rizal, the national hero of the Philippines. Unveiled on December 30, 1913, it features a bronze statue of Rizal holding a book, facing the site of his execution. The monument symbolizes Rizal's crucial role in the country's struggle for independence from Spanish colonial rule, highlighting his advocacy for reform and national identity. Surrounded by gardens and sculptures, the site serves as a significant historical and patriotic landmark, hosting commemorations and events in honor of Rizal's legacy.
